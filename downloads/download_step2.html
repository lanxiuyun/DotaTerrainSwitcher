What is a Mod?

A mod (short for "modification") is a change made by players to enhance or alter a video game. Online communities create mods to make games better, weirder, and more interesting. For example, if Farming Simulator players want old tractors not available in the base game, they create them. Similarly, you can download a mod for a new trailer skin in Euro Truck Simulator. These are just a few examples of how mods can enhance gaming experiences.
